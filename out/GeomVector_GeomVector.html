Class: GeomVector

Vector defines a direction and magnitude (size, or length) in 3D space. (Basically, an arrow pointing in a specific direction.) Represented by three numbers, referenced here as x, y, and z. Two vectors with the same magnitude and direction are the same vector. Here, angle signifies direction, in radians.
