Class: GeomPixelVector

GeomPixelVector(p)

Vector defines a direction and magnitude (size, or length) in 3D space. (Basically, an arrow pointing in a specific direction.) Represented by three numbers, referenced here as x, y, and z. For GeomPixelVector, the coordinates are integer pixel locations. Two vectors with the same magnitude and direction are the same vector. Here, angle signifies direction, in radians. Extended from Array so it can be used with math.js functions.
